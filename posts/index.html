Date: 2016 Location: UW Madison, Humanities Building, Morphy Hall Piece: Concerto in G Major Composer: Johann Joachim Quantz Accompanist: Vince Fuh This was my first WYSO concerto competition performance (I later competed in the 2018 competition). WYSO Concerto Competition 2018

Date: 2018 Location: UW Madison, Humanities Building, Morphy Hall Piece: In Ireland Composer: Hamilton Harty Accompanist: Vince Fuh This was my second WYSO concerto competition performance (I previously competed in the 2016 competition). I won this competition, alongside 3 other musicians Grace Kim, Benjamin Davies Hudson, and Ellen Zhou. I would later perform In Ireland with the Wisconsin Chamber Orchestra on May 21, 2019.
