WYSO Concerto Competition 2018

May 27, 2019

Date: 2018

Location: UW Madison, Humanities Building, Morphy Hall

Piece: In Ireland

Composer: Hamilton Harty

Accompanist: Vince Fuh

This was my second WYSO concerto competition performance (I previously competed in the 2016 competition). I won this competition, alongside 3 other musicians Grace Kim, Benjamin Davies Hudson, and Ellen Zhou. I would later perform In Ireland with the Wisconsin Chamber Orchestra on May 21, 2019.
